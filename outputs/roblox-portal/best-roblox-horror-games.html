Horror Rankings

Best Roblox horror games to play with friends

Roblox horror can range from mild chase games to intense jump scares. The best horror pages should tell players what kind of fear to expect before they join.

Top picks

  1. Doors - strong co-op tension, monster cues, and high scare level.
  2. Rainbow Friends - accessible objectives and medium scare pressure.
  3. Evade - chase intensity, team revives, and short rounds.

Parent review notes

Check jump scares, chase audio, darkness, and whether the game is still fun with known friends only. Spending pressure is usually less important than intensity.
