Parents

Know what your child is playing before they join.

Roblox is not one game. It is a platform with thousands of experiences, so every title needs its own quick safety review.

5-minute review

  1. Check the game's age guide and scare level.
  2. Look for trading, gifting, or value systems.
  3. Review chat reliance and private-server options.
  4. Search for codes only on trusted pages.
  5. Set spending rules before the first session.

Good starter picks

  1. Natural Disaster Survival
  2. Tower of Hell
  3. Brookhaven RP with known friends
  4. Theme Park Tycoon 2
  5. Restaurant Tycoon 2
Question

Is this game free-player friendly?

Look for whether purchases are cosmetic, convenience-based, or tied to progression power.

Question

Does this game require chat?

Some games are playable silently. Social roleplay games need more active supervision.

Question

Could my child be scammed?

Trading systems and fake code pages are the most common risk zones.

Question

Is horror content intense?

Jump scares, sound design, chase pressure, and darkness matter more than the label.
