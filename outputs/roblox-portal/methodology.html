Methodology

How BlockRadar scores Roblox games

The score is not a moral label. It is a practical pre-play checklist for players and parents.

Score inputs

  1. Chat risk: how much the game depends on stranger interaction.
  2. Spending pressure: whether purchases affect progress, status, or fairness.
  3. Scam exposure: trading systems, fake code searches, outside-link pressure.
  4. Scare or conflict intensity: horror, chase pressure, weapon themes, and audio intensity.
  5. Clarity: whether a new player or parent can understand the loop quickly.

Score bands

8.5+ means generally parent-friendly after normal Roblox account controls. 6.0-8.4 means review first. Below 6.0 means the game needs extra caution or a narrower audience.
