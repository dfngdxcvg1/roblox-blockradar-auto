Best Roblox games for 8 year olds

For 8 year olds, the safest starts usually have clear goals, lower scare content, limited trading pressure, and gameplay that parents can understand quickly.

First picks

  1. Theme Park Tycoon 2 - creative building and calmer play.
  2. Work at a Pizza Place - simple teamwork and social jobs.
  3. Natural Disaster Survival - short rounds with easy goals.
  4. Restaurant Tycoon 2 - management and layout planning.
  5. Brookhaven RP - relaxed roleplay, best with privacy settings reviewed.

Check before playing

Parents should review chat settings, spending permissions, friend requests, and server behavior. Use safe Roblox games for kids for more options.
