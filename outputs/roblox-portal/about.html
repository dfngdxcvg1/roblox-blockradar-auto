About

BlockRadar is a Roblox discovery and safety portal concept.

It is built around one idea: players should be able to find good games, real guides, code information, and safety context in one place.

For players

Find games by genre, free-player friendliness, friend play, codes, and beginner difficulty.

For parents

Understand chat risk, spending pressure, scare intensity, scam exposure, and age suitability.

For creators

Submit quality games for editorial review without turning discovery into pay-to-win ranking.

Methodology

Safety scores are based on chat risk, spending pressure, scam exposure, scare intensity, and gameplay clarity.
