Safety Guide

Roblox scam warning guide

Most Roblox scams use urgency, fake rewards, or outside links. A useful fan site should help players identify risk before they click.

Red flags

  1. Any page claiming to generate free Robux.
  2. Any code page asking for your Roblox password.
  3. Any trade that requires trust before the official trade window.
  4. Any player pushing you to Discord, forms, surveys, or downloads for rewards.
  5. Any giveaway using pressure like "limited time" or "prove your account".

Safe habits

Use official game links, never share passwords or one-time codes, do trades inside supported Roblox systems, and treat outside reward pages as suspicious by default.
