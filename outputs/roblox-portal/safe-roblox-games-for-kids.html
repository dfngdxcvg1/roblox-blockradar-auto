Parent Guide

Safer Roblox games for kids

Roblox safety depends on the individual game. Parents should check chat reliance, spending pressure, trading systems, scare intensity, and whether the game is understandable after a short preview.

Good starter candidates

  1. Natural Disaster Survival - simple rounds and low monetization pressure.
  2. Theme Park Tycoon 2 - creative building with low stranger reliance.
  3. Work at a Pizza Place - readable teamwork and classic roleplay.
  4. Tower of Hell - low scam risk, but can be frustrating.
  5. Restaurant Tycoon 2 - calm building and management play.

Parent checklist

Before approving a game, check the official game page, join for five minutes, review chat behavior, look for trading or gifting systems, and set Robux spending expectations.

Games needing extra review

Horror games, trading-heavy games, social roleplay games, and competitive shooters should be reviewed more carefully because the risk depends on the server and social context.
